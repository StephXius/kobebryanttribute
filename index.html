Kobe Bryant

NBA All-Star with 5 championships

Here's a timeline of Kobe Bryant's life:

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
  • 1996 - Kobe Bryant was drafted into the Charlottle Hornets and then traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 1997 - Kobe Bryant wins the Slam Dunk Contest.
  • 1999-2002 - Phil Jackson joins the Lakers making the lakers earn three titles in a row.
  • 2004 - Kobe Bryant and Shaquille O'Neal had a fallout which resulted in Shaq leaving the lakers and Kobe re-signing with the lakers. Phill Jackson also leaves the lakers.
  • 2005 - Phill Jackson joins the lakers again and Kobe drops 61 points against the Mavericks.
  • 2006 - Kobe drops a career high 81 points against the Toronto Raptors on Jan. 22 2006.
  • 2008 - Kobe becomes an Olympian and wins gold medal in the Summer games in Bejing that same year.
  • 2008-2010 - Kobe went on to win 2 more championships with the lakers. One in 2009 against the Orlando Magic and one in 2010 against the Boston Celtics.
  • 2012 - Kobe would appear in his final Olympics game. He won in the 2012 Summer Olympics game granting him his final gold medal.
  • 2016 - Kobe would go on to announce his retirement in his poem that he published "Dear Basketball" and play his final game on April. 13 2016 against the Utah Jazz. Kobe dropped 60 points in his final game.
  • 2017 - Kobe's jersey numbers 8 and 24 was retired on Dec. 18 2017.
  • 2018 - Kobe Bryant made an animated short flim of his poem "Dear Basketball" and won the Oscar for Best Animated short flim at the 90th Academy Awards. The film also took home a Sports Emmy and the Annie Award that year.
  • 2020 - The morning of Jan. 26, 2020, Kobe Bryant and the eight others on board passed away due to a helicopter crash. Kobe Bryant death shooked the world. It hurt many fans and past and current NBA players.
